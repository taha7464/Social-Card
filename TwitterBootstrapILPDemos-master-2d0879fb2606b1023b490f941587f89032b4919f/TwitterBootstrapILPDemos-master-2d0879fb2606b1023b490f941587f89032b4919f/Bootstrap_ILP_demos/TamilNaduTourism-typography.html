Tour of Tamil Nadu- The Land of Vedas.

Tamil Nadu, literally The Land of Tamils (தமிழர்) or Tamil Country) is one of the 29 states of India. Its capital and largest city is Chennai (formerly known as Madras).

WIKIPEDIA

Location

Located in south India, TN is bounded in the north by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka, in the south by the Indian Ocean, in the east by the Bay of Bengal, and by Kerala in the west.

Fairs & Festivals

The main festival of the state is Pongal, which is a harvest festival celebrated in the month of January. Feasting, music and dance mark this festival. Another festival, Thaipusam, celebrated in the months of January and February in Thanjavur and Palani, is marked by ritual bathing.
