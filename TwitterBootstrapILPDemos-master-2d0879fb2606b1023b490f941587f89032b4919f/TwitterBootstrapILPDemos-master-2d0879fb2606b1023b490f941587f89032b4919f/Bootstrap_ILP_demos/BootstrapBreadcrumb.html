Tour of Tamil Nadu- The Land of Vedas

Location

Located in south India, TN is bounded in the north by Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka, in the south by the Indian Ocean, in the east by the Bay of Bengal, and by Kerala in the west. It is the 11th largest state in India, circumscribing the union territory of Pondicherry in the district of South Arcot.

Fairs & Festivals

The main festival of the state is Pongal, which is a harvest festival celebrated in the month of January. Feasting, music and dance mark this festival. Another festival, Thaipusam, celebrated in the months of January and February in Thanjavur and Palani, is marked by ritual bathing. The Velankanni Festival, held at Velankanni in August/September, is held in honor of Virgin Mary, who is regarded to have miraculous healing powers. Besides these, Navratri, Deepavali, Christmas, New Year's Day are also celebrated with traditional gaiety and fervor.
