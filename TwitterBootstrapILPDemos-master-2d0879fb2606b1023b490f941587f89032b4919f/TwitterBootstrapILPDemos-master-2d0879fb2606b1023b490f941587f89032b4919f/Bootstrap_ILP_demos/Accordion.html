Remarkable India

FAQs

When can I avail my first Remarkable India Holiday?

Once your reservation is confirmed, you will get a confirmation voucher. You can avail this voucher when you check-in.

How do I book my Holidays?

Log in on the Home page of the website www.remarkableindia.com

How do I pay the subsription fee?

You can pay online (website/IVRS).

What is the Cancellation Policy?

A cancellation fee of Rs. 1,000 will be charged if a Member cancels a confirmed holiday.

What documents should I carry with me when checking in?

Furnishing the confirmation voucher, your membership card and photo identity card is a must at the time of check in.
